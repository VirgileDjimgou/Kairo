# Commercialization Notes

These notes define a simple commercial offer structure for Kairo.

## Offer Shapes

### 1. Self-hosted license + support

- Customer hosts Kairo on their own infrastructure.
- The delivery team provides onboarding, upgrades, and support under a service contract.
- Best for organizations with internal IT or a preferred hosting partner.

### 2. Managed service

- Kairo is hosted and operated by the delivery team or a managed infrastructure partner.
- Backups, upgrades, and incident handling are part of the service.
- Best for customers who want outcomes rather than infrastructure ownership.

### 3. Pilot / evaluation package

- Short-term deployment for a proof-of-value or pilot tenant.
- Includes onboarding, limited support, and clear success criteria.
- Converts into a self-hosted or managed-service agreement when validated.

## Pricing Structure Example

This is a structure, not a final price list:

- one-time setup and onboarding fee
- recurring support or hosting fee
- optional data migration fee
- optional training fee
- optional custom integration fee

## Boundary Rules

- Core product features are not confused with service work.
- Support SLAs, infrastructure responsibilities, and data ownership are explicit.
- Optional channels and future connectors are not sold as already integrated.
- Identity email delivery can be presented as available, and operator notifications can now be presented as available through SMTP, Telegram, and a gateway-backed WhatsApp path. Provider-side reconciliation and final-state guarantees must still be presented as future work.

## First-Contact Materials

For a first discussion with an association, prepare:

- the short offer pack
- the buyer FAQ
- the feature matrix
- the demo-to-production checklist
- the support playbook

## Business Decisions Still Needed

- final currency and pricing model
- support hours and response expectations
- SLA wording for managed service
- legal terms and data processing agreements
- branding and market positioning by target segment
