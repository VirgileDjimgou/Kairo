# Commercial Packaging

This folder collects the customer-facing and handoff-facing material for the final commercial packaging track through Sprint 65.

## What Kairo Is

Kairo, also positioned as OrgMind AI, is a local-first, multi-tenant RAG platform for organizations that need:

- private document search and question answering with citations
- tenant isolation enforced by the backend
- role-based access control for members, staff, and admins
- operational workflows for documents, members, contributions, events, policies, and audit trails

## Commercial Positioning

The product can be presented in two simple packaging modes:

### Self-hosted edition

- Customer runs Kairo on their own infrastructure
- Customer owns uptime, backups, and infrastructure operation
- Open-ended customization is possible within the documented architecture

### Managed-service edition

- Kairo is operated by the delivery team or a hosting partner
- Backups, upgrades, and incident response are included in the support contract
- The customer focuses on data ownership and usage rather than platform operation

## What This Folder Must Achieve

After Sprint 65, a new evaluator should be able to understand quickly:

- what Kairo is for
- who it is for
- what is included right now
- what is service work rather than product scope
- what remains explicitly out of scope
- how to move from demo to production
- where support begins and ends
