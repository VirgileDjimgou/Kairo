# Public Entry Surface

This document describes the public-facing login page after Sprint 26.

## Purpose

The login page is not just an authentication form anymore. It is the first place a visitor sees the product value.

## Message Hierarchy

1. Kairo is a local-first, multi-tenant RAG platform.
2. Tenant isolation is enforced by the backend.
3. Answers are grounded in citations.
4. The platform supports operational workflows, not only document chat.
5. The product is deployable with a validated production posture.

## What The Page Should Communicate

- private AI for organizations
- document intelligence with citations
- member and operational workflows
- production readiness and recoverability
- self-hosted and managed-service offer shapes

## What The Page Must Not Do

- expose tenant data
- require a login to understand the basics
- distract from the sign-in flow
- claim features that are still placeholders

## Maintenance Rule

If future product positioning changes, update this page alongside the login view and the commercial feature matrix.
